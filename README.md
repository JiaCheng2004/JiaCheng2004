## What I Ship

> Every project below is architected, tested, deployed, and documented. No tutorials. No toy apps.

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### [Polaris](https://github.com/JiaCheng2004/Polaris)
**Stateless Multi-Modal AI Gateway**

Config-driven gateway routing requests across **29 providers** (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Bedrock, ByteDance, DeepSeek, Mistral, Ollama, and more) with multi-modal support — chat, video, audio, music, images, embeddings, realtime audio sessions. Built-in control plane for projects, virtual keys, policies, budgets, and MCP bindings. Graceful degradation: SQLite → PostgreSQL, in-memory → Redis, provider failover chains.

`Go` `Gin` `PostgreSQL` `SQLite` `Redis` `Prometheus` `OpenTelemetry` `WebSocket` `Docker`

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### [AniClips](https://github.com/JiaCheng2004/AniClips)
**Anime Short Video Platform**

Cassandra-backed media ingest with idempotent writes and background transcoding. Cloudflare CDN edge delivery with signed URLs — **92% cache hit ratio**. SwiftUI gesture feed with prefetching and backpressure — dropped frames under 1% on mid-tier iPhones. p99 player startup under **320 ms** at 10k RPS.

`Swift` `SwiftUI` `Spring Boot` `Cassandra` `Cloudflare CDN` `S3` `XCTest`

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### [BlinkPad](https://github.com/JiaCheng2004/BlinkPad)
**Real-Time Collaborative Notepad**

Anonymous collaborative editing with **CRDT-based sync**. Lexical rich-text editor + Yjs, Go WebSocket backend, room-based sessions. Deployed on single VPS with Caddy.

`Go` `React 19` `Next.js 16` `Yjs` `Lexical` `WebSocket`

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### [Nextdaytion](https://github.com/Nextdaytion/Nextdaytion)
**Online Co-op Survival Game**

Unity 6 with host-authoritative multiplayer via Netcode for GameObjects, Relay for NAT traversal, Vivox proximity voice, Addressables for async scene loading, and KINEMATION for first-person animation.

`Unity 6` `C#` `Netcode for GameObjects` `Relay` `Vivox` `KINEMATION`
